Harness Size Guide

Finding the perfect fit can be tricky. Luckily, just like dogs, our Walk gear comes in all different sizes. Use the guides below to find the size best suited to your pup!

How to Measure

Other Sizing Tips

1. Don't have a soft tape measure? Use a piece of string or rope and measure the string with a ruler as you go.

2. For growing puppies or dogs with extra thick coats, we recommend adding a little extra space (3 fingers) when measuring.

3. In between sizes? Our recommendation is to size up!
